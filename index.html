Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how QSApp ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use QSApp:

  • Account information: email address and password when you register.
  • Project data: drawings, scope of works, and cost estimates you create within the app.
  • Usage data: API usage logs to monitor service performance and costs.
  • Address/postcode data: used solely to determine the regional pricing factor for your estimate. Not stored beyond the current session.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the QSApp service.
  • To generate cost estimates using AI (Anthropic Claude API). Uploaded drawings and scope text are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.
  • To store your projects securely so you can access them across devices.
  • To monitor usage and maintain service reliability.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform. Data is stored on servers within the European Union. See Supabase's Privacy Policy for further information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with the third-party services required to operate the app:

  • Anthropic — for AI-powered scope extraction from uploaded drawings.
  • Supabase — for secure data storage and authentication.

5. Data Retention

Your account and project data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

8. Children's Privacy

QSApp is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
